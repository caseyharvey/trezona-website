about us

We are a Melbourne-based social purpose management consultancy, specialising in public health and social policy across the health, community, not-for-profit and government sectors. Our key work areas include evaluation, research, strategy development, stakeholder engagement and resource development. We are committed to promoting equity, social justice and human rights through our work and partnerships.

We acknowledge the Wurundjeri Woi Wurrung People of the Kulin Nation as the sovereign custodians of the land on which we work, and recognise their continuing connection with their lands. We pay our respects to their Elders past and present.
